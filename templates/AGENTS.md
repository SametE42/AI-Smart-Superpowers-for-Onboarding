## Core rules

- Follow `/docs/ai/` as the source of truth.
- Do not invent business rules.
- Do not expose secrets or sensitive data.
- Do not make destructive changes without explicit human approval.
- Before code changes, propose a short plan.
- After changes, run the relevant verification step or document why it was not run.
- Keep changes small and scoped.
